WHO WE ARE

About Aeltrix XR

Redefining how brands connect with audiences through immersive digital experiences.

At Aeltrix XR, we are redefining how brands connect with their audiences through immersive digital experiences. By combining the power of Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), and Extended Reality (XR), we help businesses transform traditional customer interactions into engaging, interactive, and memorable experiences.

Whether it's enabling customers to virtually try on jewelry, fashion, eyewear, or accessories, creating lifelike 3D product visualizations, building virtual showrooms, or developing immersive advertising campaigns, our solutions bridge the gap between the physical and digital worlds. We empower brands to increase customer engagement, boost conversion rates, and deliver next-generation shopping experiences.

Founded by a team of passionate fourth-year B.Tech students from VIT Pune, Aeltrix XR was born from a shared vision of bringing cutting-edge immersive technology to businesses of all sizes. With strong expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, AR/VR Development, and 3D Visualization, we are building innovative solutions that help brands stay ahead in the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Our mission is simple: to make online shopping more interactive, product discovery more engaging, and brand experiences more immersive than ever before. We believe the future of commerce lies in experiential technology, where customers don't just view products — they experience them.

From virtual try-ons and interactive 3D product showcases to immersive marketing campaigns and virtual brand experiences, Aeltrix XR is helping businesses unlock the full potential of the next generation of digital engagement.
